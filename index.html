LET'S LEARN MORE ABOUT TENNIS

Tennis has its origins in the tennis game that appeared in medieval France. This sport had been invented by monks to make physical exercise then it was taken up by the richest. Two players were separated by a net, and returned a leather ball. Then, rackets appaeared and it's only in the 19th century that the word "tennis" appeared. Before, the server used to announce its sending by shouting "Tenez" and in english, it translates to "Tenèts" or "Tennis". The first tournament was born in England, at Wimbledon in 1877 and 5 years later, the French Open at Roland-Garros was born. In 1968, the modern and professional tennis was born with the beginning of the Open era. Today, the Grand Chelem tournaments are 4 major tournaments very famous : ROLAND GARROS in may-june, US OPEN in august-september, AUSTRALIA OPEN in january, WIMBLEDON in july.
